About Me

I’m Paul Michael Santos, but just call me Paul. Born in Staten Island, grew up in New Jersey (don’t ask me why), living life like a New Yorker. I went to Rutgers University and completed my Bachelor’s Degree in Biomedical Engineering. After graduating I started working as a lab technician at a medical device company. Lab coat, gloves and goggles. Yes. Lab coats and goggles. I worked hard and worked my way up from a lab coat wearing technician to a lab coat wearing engineer. With gloves and goggles. As cool as it sounds, wearing goggles at work doesn’t excite me. Turns out there’s more to life than working like Mr. White. One day my cousin told me about a startup idea that he had and asked me to work with him and build a site for him. I began my journey on CodeAcademy and I was hooked. I decided I needed guidance and enrolled in the Rutgers Coding Bootcamp.

I was chillin’ with my cousin from down under(Australia) when he told me about an “idea”. Of course like everyone else, a “Startup Idea”. Yes. They think about that in Australia too, not limited to Silicon Valley. So I started this journey on CodeAcademy and I. Was. Hooked. That’s when I decided I needed to accelerate my learning. I need my learning curve to hockey-stick (that’s right Silicon Valley lingo right there) and enrolled in the Rutgers Coding Bootcamp.

On a more personal note, I love to ride my motorcycles and enjoy the open road. I have owned a Ducati Monster, an MV Agusta F3, and a Suzuki DRZ400sm. For those who don’t know anything about bikes, I have owned a very fast black motorcycle, a crazy fast red motorcycle and a slow but insanely fun motorcycle. I also love sports, I follow anything with NY on their name but mainly NY Knicks, NY Mets, NY rangers. If feel like you want to know more about me hit me up on the contact page.
