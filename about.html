About Me

My name is Paul Santos. I received my education from Rutgers University with a bachelors in Biomedical Engineering. Sounds exciting doesn't it? After graduating, I started as a lab technician at a medical device company and worked my way up to become an engineer. Turns out that I don't enjoy engineering as much as I thought I would. One day my cousin told me about a startup idea that he had and asked me to work with him and build a site for him. I began my journey on CodeAcademy and I was hooked. I decided I needed guidance and enrolled in the Rutgers Coding Bootcamp.

I was born in Staten Island, NY but grew up in New Jersey. (I still like to say I'm a New Yorker)

I love to ride my motorcycles and pretend I'm Valentino Rossi. I have owned a Ducati Monster, MV Agusta F3, and a Suzuki DRZ400sm. My sports teams are the NY Knicks, NY Mets, NY Rangers. I also like to box as a hobby.
